About Us

MFM Standard Nursery and Primary School is an institution dedicated to providing top-quality early childhood and basic education in a God-fearing, nurturing, and stimulating environment.

Our Mission

To raise morally upright and academically sound pupils by offering quality education guided by the fear of God.

Our Vision

To become a leading school in Niger State known for excellence in character, knowledge, and discipline.

Our Motto

"O' God Increase Me in Knowledge."
